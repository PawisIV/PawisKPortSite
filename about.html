About Me

I’m Pawis Kitsanawan, a Game Designer from Thailand specializing in gameplay systems, level design, and player experience. I focus on designing engaging interactions and polished mechanics through prototyping and teamwork.

I enjoy bringing ideas to life, solving design problems creatively, and collaborating with the team to build fun, memorable player experiences.
