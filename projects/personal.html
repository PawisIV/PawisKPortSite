Overview

Apex Predator is a third-person shooter that mixes gunplay and close combat into smooth, fast-paced Gun-Fu action. Use guns and melee moves together to chain attacks, take down enemies, and execute them to show your skill in stylish, deadly fights.

The game’s narrative unfolds across six distinct levels, each designed as a key chapter in the overarching tale of Nero’s Revenge. These levels, referred to as Acts, guide players through an escalating journey of intense combat, cinematic action, and pivotal story moments, with each Act bringing Nero one step closer to his ultimate goal.

Design Goals
  • Integrated Combat System: Develop a seamless blend of close-quarters combat and third-person shooting. This system rewards a high-risk, high-efficiency playstyle, creating a unique and exciting experience that distinguishes the game from others in the market.
  • Narrative-Driven Environments: Design maps and atmospheres that act as silent storytellers. Every location is crafted to help players naturally understand the game’s world and narrative through environmental cues.
  • Intense and Organic Encounters: Create combat encounters that feel natural yet intense. This is achieved by combining level design with reactive enemy behaviors to keep players engaged and challenged.

Level Design Framwork

Linear Map Flowchart

Linear Map Low intensity

  • Have fighting action that doesn’t require positioning skill to clear
  • Only require shooting & Gun-fu Basic Skill
  • Doesn’t require positioning skill
  • Straight Line combat

Linear Map High intensity

  • Have fighting action that require positioning skill and gunfight skill to clear
  • Require more understanding of shooting & Gun-fu Skill
  • Require Player positioning
  • Straight Line combat with sub route

Circuit Map Flowchart

Circuit Map

  • Have fighting action that require seriously positioning skill and gunfight to clear
  • Require fully understanding of shooting & Gun-fu Skill
  • Require Player positioning and game sense
  • Enemy AI moving and make decision in group
  • Open combat area, create varie of combat style
